Reporting Guidelines

Help us keep our community safe and fun for everyone! Please follow these steps when reporting an issue:

  1. Be Specific: Clearly describe the issue, including usernames, game names, or links if relevant.
  2. Provide Evidence: Attach screenshots or videos if possible to help us understand the problem.
  3. Stay Respectful: Use polite language and avoid personal attacks.
  4. Do Not Spam: Submit your report once. Multiple reports for the same issue may delay our response.
  5. Follow Up: If you have more information, reply to your original report instead of creating a new one.

Thank you for helping us maintain a positive environment!
